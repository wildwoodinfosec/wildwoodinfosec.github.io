Wildwood InfoSec provides penetration testing and adversary-minded security assessments focused on high-signal findings and real remediation outcomes.

## How We Work
- We start with your objectives and threat model
- We test like an attacker (not a scanner report)
- We prioritize findings that materially reduce risk
- We deliver clear, reproducible write-ups your engineers can act on

## What You Can Expect
- Professional communication and tight scoping
- Minimal operational disruption
- Actionable reporting with clear priorities
- Optional retesting to confirm fixes
